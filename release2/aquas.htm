Aqua S

MENU ITEMS

If you love authentic and unique soft serve ice cream, then you have found the right place! Aqua S is the perfect spot for a small, yet colorfully tasteful dessert. The restaurant has comfortable seating, with picture-worthy decorations that turn your dessert into an experience. I really enjoyed their sea salt soft-serve ice cream. They also have mini tub in multiple creative flavors that are the perfect size!

Price: The prices are fairly reasonable. The mini tubs range between $3-$5, while the soft-serve ice cream is around $5.

Address: 2375 Victory ParkLn Ste 140 Dallas, TX 75219
