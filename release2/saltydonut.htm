The Salty Donut

MENU ITEMS

The Salty Donut is another amazing coffee shop located in the Bishop Arts District. The shop has plenty of indoor and outdoor seating. I really enjoyed this place as they are dog-friendly, as are most of the shops around the area. The Salty Donut makes the most instagrmmable donuts that are a delicious treat to accompany thier coffee. If you are looking for a super sweet coffee, I would definitely recommend the Cinnamon Toast Crunch latte, which is as amazing as it sounds.

Price: $ Prices are fairly low.

Address: 414 W Davis St Dallas, TX 75208

Phone Number: (972) 707-9889
