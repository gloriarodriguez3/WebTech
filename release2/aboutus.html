About Us

Get To Know Me

Hi, my name is Gloria Rodriguez! I am super excited to share my favorite dessert and coffee spots in the beautiful city of Dallas, Texas. I decided to make this website because I discovered many places in Dallas with my boyfriend during the COVID-19 pandemic. As a result of the pandemic, my boyfriend and I were forced to continue our studies at home and that is when we decided to make it our mission to find new places to study and treat ourselves with dessert. Naturally, we gravitated towards coffee shops that were suitable for studying. However, we did also include places that were great for hanging out after a nice hike, or even for a quick morning coffee run before the start of our online classes. We thoroughly enjoyed all of the shops included in this website and we would highly recommend others to visit as well!
